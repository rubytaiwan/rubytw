眾所矚目的 RubyElixirConf Taiwan 2018 已確定將在 2018 年 4 月 27~28 日於張榮發基金會國際會議中心召開，本屆大會將加入 Elixir 語言並將於 9.19 開放投稿，相關資訊將會陸續在[大會官網](https://2018.rubyconf.tw/)公佈。

We are proud to announce the upcoming RubyElixirConf 2018 which is going to take place in CHANG YUNG-FA FOUNDATION International Convention Center, Taipei from 27th to 28th April. Beside Ruby, the topics of Elixir will be present this time. For up-to-date information please visit the following links: 
 
- Conference Official Site：https://2018.rubyconf.tw/
- Call for Proposals（Will open 9.19）：https://cfp.rubyconf.tw/activities/2018 
  
Please mail to contact@rubyconf.tw for any question of sponsorship.
